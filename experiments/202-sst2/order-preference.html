Instructions

In this experiment, you will be rewriting sentences.

You will see short movie reviews. Your task is to change it so that it sounds positive or negative.


There will be an example before the start of the experiment.

The goal of the experiment is to understand what differentiates positive from negative reviews, and to improve computers' ability to understand sentiment.

Our system will analyze your responses on a few test sentences, and alert you in case you're doing the task incorrectly.


We invite you to participate in a research study on language production and comprehension. Your experimenter will ask you to do a linguistic task such as reading sentences or words, naming pictures or describing scenes, making up sentences of your own, or participating in a simple language game.
