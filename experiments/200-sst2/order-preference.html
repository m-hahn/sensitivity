Instructions

You will read short movie reviews.

For each review, you will be asked whether it describes a positive or negative attitude to the movie on the whole.

At the beginning of the study, there are seven example items with explanations.

Don't worry if you're not 100% sure about the right answer, and just select the option that you intuitively prefer.
However, we will monitor your responses on a series of attention check paragraphs where the answer is obvious and unambiguous. We'll alert you if you make mistakes on those.
